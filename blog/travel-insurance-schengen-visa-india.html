Do You Really Need Travel Insurance for a Schengen Visa?

March 2026 · 5 min read · By Policy Aid

Yes — Schengen travel insurance is a mandatory visa requirement for all 27 Schengen countries. Without it, your visa application will be rejected. Here's everything you need to know.

🇪🇺 Schengen countries include: France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Switzerland, Greece, Portugal, Austria, Belgium and 17 more. All require the same minimum insurance coverage.

Minimum Requirements

  • Minimum medical coverage: €30,000 (approximately ₹27 Lakh)
  • Must cover emergency medical treatment, hospitalisation and repatriation
  • Must be valid for the entire Schengen zone (not just one country)
  • Must be valid for your entire trip duration plus 15 days buffer

What Our Schengen Plans Cover

  • Emergency dental treatment
  • Emergency medical evacuation and repatriation
  • Trip cancellation due to illness or emergency
  • Lost baggage and delayed luggage
  • Flight delays over 6 hours
