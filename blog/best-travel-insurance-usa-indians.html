Best Travel Insurance for Indians Visiting the USA in 2026

February 2026 · 8 min read · By Policy Aid

A single night in a US hospital can cost $10,000–$50,000. For Indians visiting the USA — whether for tourism, business, visiting family or medical treatment — comprehensive travel insurance is not optional. It's essential.

🚨 Without travel insurance, a medical emergency in the USA can result in bills of ₹50 lakh or more — and US hospitals can pursue debt collection in India.

Why US Travel Insurance is Different

  • No public health system — every treatment is privately billed
  • Emergency room visits start at $1,000+ even for minor issues
  • Ambulance rides cost $2,000–$3,000
  • Medical evacuation back to India can cost $50,000+

What to Look for in US Travel Insurance

  • Minimum $1,00,000 medical cover (we recommend $5,00,000)
  • Pre-existing disease cover (if applicable — declare everything)
  • COVID-19 medical cover
  • 24/7 English-language emergency helpline in the US
  • Cashless treatment at US hospitals
