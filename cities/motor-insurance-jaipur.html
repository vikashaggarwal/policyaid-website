Essential Add-Ons for Jaipur Vehicle Owners

Zero Depreciation Cover

Standard policies deduct depreciation (15–50%) from repair claims. Zero dep ensures you get the full repair cost — especially valuable for newer vehicles in Jaipur's accident-prone areas.

Engine and Gearbox Protection

This add-on is especially recommended for Jaipur given local road conditions. Engine damage from waterlogging or rough roads is NOT covered in standard policies — engine protection fills this critical gap.

Return to Invoice (RTI)

In case of total loss, RTI pays you the original purchase price (not depreciated IDV). Highly recommended for vehicles in Jaipur under 3 years old, given the city's theft and accident rates.

Jaipur tip: Add roadside assistance cover if you travel on NH-48 frequently — breakdowns and accidents on the Jaipur-Delhi highway stretch can be costly without emergency towing and repair coverage.
