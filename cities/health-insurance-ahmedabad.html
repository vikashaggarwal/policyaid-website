What to Check Before Buying Health Insurance in Ahmedabad

1. Network Hospital Coverage

Verify that your plan includes Sterling Hospitals and other hospitals in your neighbourhood. A plan with ₹5 lakh cover but poor local network can leave you paying out-of-pocket in an emergency.

2. Waiting Periods

Most plans have 2–4 year waiting periods for pre-existing diseases and 1–2 years for specific conditions. Buy health insurance when you are healthy to minimise the impact of waiting periods.

3. No-Claim Bonus (NCB)

Top plans offer 50–100% NCB on sum insured for every claim-free year. Over 5 years, your ₹5 lakh cover can grow to ₹10 lakh with zero additional premium — a critical feature for Ahmedabad residents.

4. Room Rent Limits

In Ahmedabad's top hospitals, room rents easily exceed ₹5,000–₹15,000 per day. Ensure your plan has no room rent capping or choose a plan with a 1% of sum insured daily limit to avoid proportionate deductions.
