Plan Highlights

What makes Care International Travel Insurance stand out from the competition

Pay Only for Days Travelled

Premium is charged on a strict per-day basis with no trip-band slabs, so you never overpay for a short trip.

Pre-Existing Diseases Covered (10%)

Pre-existing conditions are covered up to 10% of the principal sum insured for both accident and sickness - a rare benefit.

No Maximum Entry Age

Unlike most travel insurers, there is no upper age cap, making this plan accessible to senior citizens of any age.

Newborn Babies Eligible

Coverage is available from day 1 of birth, with no minimum age requirement for infants travelling with family.

Policy Extension up to 180 Days

If your trip runs longer than planned, the policy can be extended up to a total duration of 180 days.

High Sum Insured up to $1 Million

Who Should Buy Care International Travel Insurance?

This plan is best suited for the following profiles

Senior Citizens with No Age Limit

With no maximum entry age, this plan is one of the very few options available to elderly travellers who are turned away by other insurers.

Families with Newborns

Newborn babies are eligible for cover from birth, making this ideal for families travelling with infants.

Travellers with Pre-existing Conditions

The 10% sum insured cover for pre-existing diseases offers some protection that most competitors simply don't provide.

Short and Variable-Duration Trips

The strict per-day premium structure makes this cost-effective for short trips and flexible for travellers unsure of exact trip length.
