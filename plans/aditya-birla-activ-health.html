Plan Highlights

Activ Health is India's most wellness-integrated health insurance plan, rewarding you for staying healthy while covering chronic illnesses from day 1.

Chronic Management Program

Listed chronic conditions including diabetes, hypertension, and asthma are covered from day 1 of the policy when you enroll in the CMP — no 3-year wait for these conditions.

HealthReturns: 30% Premium Back

Earn up to 30% of your annual premium back as rewards by meeting health activity goals tracked via the Activ Health app. No other plan in India offers this.

Mental Wellness Cover

In-patient mental health treatment including psychiatric hospitalisation is covered — a progressive inclusion that most Indian health policies still exclude.

Maternity Benefit Included

Normal and caesarean delivery expenses are covered in the Platinum Enhanced plan, along with newborn cover from birth, subject to a 2-year waiting period.

Personal Accident Cover

Accidental death and permanent total disability lump sum cover is bundled into the plan, removing the need for a separate personal accident policy.

Dental Cover

Dental treatment expenses arising from an accident or illness are covered, a benefit largely absent in most standard health insurance policies.

No Room Rent Sub-limit

Any room category can be chosen during hospitalisation without triggering proportional deductions on other charges — no hidden reductions.

Wellness Coaching App

The Activ Health app provides a personalised wellness coach, step tracking, health assessments, and specialist consultations to support proactive health management.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Aditya Birla Activ Health Platinum Enhanced, answered plainly.

On policy issuance, you register for the CMP and undergo an initial health assessment. For listed chronic conditions (e.g., diabetes, hypertension, asthma), the plan waives the standard waiting period and covers hospitalisation from day 1 of the policy. You must remain enrolled in the program and meet periodic check-in requirements. Conditions not on the CMP list still follow the standard 3-year pre-existing disease wait.
HealthReturns are earned by completing health activities tracked through the Activ Health app: daily step count targets, gym visits, health screenings, nutrition tracking, and annual health check-ups. Points accumulate and are converted into a premium discount of up to 30% at renewal. The more consistently you engage with wellness activities, the higher your return. Unused rewards may carry forward partially depending on plan terms.
In-patient hospitalisation for mental health conditions including depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, and other listed psychiatric disorders is covered. Outpatient consultations and therapy sessions have limited coverage depending on the variant. As always, specific exclusions such as substance abuse or self-inflicted conditions apply. Review the policy wording for the complete list of covered and excluded mental health conditions.
The maternity benefit in Activ Health Platinum Enhanced kicks in after a 2-year waiting period from the policy start date. It covers normal delivery, caesarean section, and pre and post-natal expenses up to the specified sub-limit. Newborn cover is included from birth for the first 90 days without requiring separate enrollment. Pre-existing conditions of the newborn are also covered subject to terms.
