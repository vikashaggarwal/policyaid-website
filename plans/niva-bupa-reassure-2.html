Plan Highlights

Why Niva Bupa Reassure 2.0 is built for the long term

Lock the Clock - India's Only Age-Locked Premium Benefit

Reassure 2.0's Lock the Clock feature freezes your premium at your age of entry. Whether you buy at 28 or 40, the age-related component of your premium never increases. This is a powerful long-term benefit that saves tens of thousands of rupees over a lifetime of coverage.

Lock the Clock Premium

Your premium is locked at the age you buy the policy. No age-related premium increases - a unique long-term savings benefit.

150% No Claim Bonus

The highest NCB in India. Your sum insured grows 50% for every claim-free year, up to 150% of the original cover amount.

Unlimited Reinstatement

Sum insured restored unlimited times in a policy year, for the same or different illness. No single-restoration caps.

Direct Claim Settlement

No TPA - Niva Bupa settles all claims in-house. Cashless approvals are processed directly with the insurer's team.

International Treatment Cover

Coverage for life-threatening conditions requiring overseas hospitalisation, built into the plan as a standard feature.

Maternity and New-born Cover

Maternity hospitalisation and new-born cover from birth included after the standard waiting period.

No Room Rent Limit

Select any hospital room including premium private rooms without worrying about proportionate deductions on other charges.

No Co-payment

No co-payment clause means Niva Bupa pays 100% of eligible claim amounts. You do not share any portion of the claim.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about Niva Bupa Reassure 2.0

Lock the Clock means your health insurance premium is fixed at the age you first purchase the policy. Unlike other plans where premiums increase every few years as you age into a higher bracket, Reassure 2.0 only allows industry-wide rate revisions to affect your premium. A person buying at age 30 will always be priced as a 30-year-old for age-related components, potentially saving lakhs in premium over a lifetime.
Reassure 2.0 offers the highest NCB in the Indian market at 150%. For each claim-free year, your sum insured increases by 50% of the original base cover. After 3 consecutive claim-free years, your cover reaches its maximum at 150% above the original. So a ₹10 Lakh policy grows to ₹12.5 Lakh, then ₹15 Lakh, and finally ₹25 Lakh over 3 years - without any premium revision for the additional cover.
Yes. Reassure 2.0 includes international treatment cover for life-threatening conditions and major surgeries that cannot be adequately treated within India. This covers the actual cost of hospitalisation abroad, subject to the sum insured. This is one of very few Indian health plans that provide genuine overseas hospitalisation coverage as a standard feature rather than an add-on rider.
Unlimited reinstatement means that if your entire sum insured is exhausted in a policy year, it gets restored 100% - and this can happen an unlimited number of times within the same policy year, for the same or different illnesses. Most plans offer at most one restoration per year. Reassure 2.0's unlimited feature is especially valuable for families with severe or recurring health conditions that may require multiple hospitalisations in a year.
