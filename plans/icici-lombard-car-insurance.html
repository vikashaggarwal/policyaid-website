Plan Highlights

What makes ICICI Lombard Private Car Package Policy stand out

6,800+ Network Garages

Wide cashless garage network across India ensures convenient access to repairs near you, in metros and smaller towns alike.

Engine Protect Plus

Comprehensive engine cover extending to gearbox and differential, going beyond standard engine protect add-ons offered elsewhere.

Roadside Assistance (4x/Year)

Up to 4 roadside assistance call-outs per policy year covering towing, breakdown support, fuel delivery and minor repairs.

Zero Depreciation Add-on

Ensures full claim value on replaced parts without depreciation deductions, ideal for cars under 5 years old.

Return to Invoice (RTI)

Get the full invoice value of your car including registration and road tax in case of total loss or theft.

Multiple Discount Options

NCB discount plus additional discounts for anti-theft devices and AAI (Automobile Association of India) membership.

Key Protect & Garage Cash

Covers cost of lost car key replacement, plus daily cash allowance while your car is under repair at a network garage.

Pay As You Drive Option

Usage-based insurance variant for low-mileage drivers who want premiums linked to actual vehicle usage.

Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of ICICI Lombard Private Car Package Policy

Advantages

  • Wide network of 6,800+ cashless garages across India
  • Engine Protect Plus covers gearbox and differential, not just the engine
  • 24/7 customer support for claims and queries
  • Multiple discount avenues - NCB, anti-theft devices, AAI membership
  • Highly customisable with add-ons like Garage Cash and Key Protect
  • Roadside assistance available up to 4 times per policy year
  • Pay As You Drive option for low-mileage drivers

Disadvantages

  • Claim settlement ratio (~92.9%) is lower than HDFC ERGO, Digit or Bajaj Allianz
  • Premium is slightly above the segment average
  • Roadside assistance is capped at 4 call-outs per year, unlike unlimited options elsewhere
  • Many valuable features require add-on purchases, increasing overall cost
