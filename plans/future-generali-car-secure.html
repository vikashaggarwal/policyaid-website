Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of Future Generali Car Secure

Advantages

  • Competitive, budget-friendly premium (~₹4,600/year)
  • Cashless settlement at 3,000+ network garages
  • Hassle-free, quick claims process with minimal paperwork
  • All essential add-ons available - Zero Dep, Engine Protect, NCB Protect, RTI
  • Personal accident cover included as standard
  • Standalone OD and Third-Party variants also available
  • Good fit for cost-conscious buyers wanting solid basic coverage

Disadvantages

  • Claim settlement ratio of 93% is slightly below the industry average of 94.67%
  • Smaller garage network (3,000+) compared to SBI General, Reliance or Tata AIG
  • Fewer specialised add-ons compared to Tata AIG or ICICI Lombard
  • Less brand recognition compared to larger private insurers
