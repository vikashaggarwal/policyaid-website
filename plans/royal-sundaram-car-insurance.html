Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of Royal Sundaram Car Insurance

Advantages

  • Lowest comprehensive premium among the plans compared (~₹4,400/year)
  • Full-time customer assistance including roadside support
  • Decent garage network of 3,300+ across India
  • Quick and assisted claims process
  • All standard add-ons available - Zero Dep, RTI, Engine Protect, NCB Protect
  • Standalone OD and Third-Party variants also available
  • Good entry-level option for budget-focused buyers

Disadvantages

  • Incurred claim ratio data (~85%, as of Nov 2019) is older and may not reflect current performance
  • Smaller garage network compared to SBI General, Reliance or Tata AIG
  • Fewer specialised/niche add-ons compared to ICICI Lombard or Tata AIG
  • Lower premium may mean fewer bundled benefits compared to higher-priced plans
