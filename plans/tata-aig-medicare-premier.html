Plan Highlights

Medicare Premier is a premium plan built for those who want comprehensive coverage including international treatment.

International Treatment Cover

Medical treatment outside India for serious conditions is covered, a rare inclusion that sets Medicare Premier apart from most domestic plans.

Air Ambulance Included

Air ambulance charges for emergency evacuation are covered in the base plan — no additional rider required, unlike most competitors.

Organ Donor Expenses

Covers the medical expenses of the organ donor incurred during the harvesting procedure, reducing the financial burden of transplant surgeries.

Critical Illness Lump Sum

On diagnosis of a listed critical illness, a lump sum payout is made in addition to hospitalisation coverage — dual protection.

Premium Waiver on CI Diagnosis

If you are diagnosed with a critical illness, future premiums are waived while the policy continues in full force. This is an exceptional benefit.

Home Healthcare

Post-discharge home healthcare is covered when recommended by a treating doctor, supporting continued recovery without readmission costs.

NCB up to 100%

Cumulative no-claim bonus builds year over year up to 100% of the base sum insured, rewarding policy holders who stay healthy.

Room Rent: Single AC Only

The room rent limit is capped at a single AC room. Upgrading to a higher category results in proportional cuts across all hospital charges.

Who Should Buy Medicare Premier?

This plan is designed for specific buyer profiles where its premium features justify the higher cost.

High-Net-Worth Individuals

The ₹3 Crore sum insured and international treatment coverage make this plan ideal for those who want elite-level protection without limits.

Frequent International Travellers

International treatment cover within the base plan removes the need for a separate international health insurance policy for medical emergencies abroad.

People with Family History of Critical Illness

The dual critical illness benefit — lump sum payout plus premium waiver — offers powerful financial protection for those at elevated risk.

Those Considering Organ Transplant Risk

With organ donor expenses covered, this plan is among the few that reduce the full financial burden of transplant surgeries on the insured.
