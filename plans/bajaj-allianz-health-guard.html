Plan Highlights

What makes Bajaj Allianz Health Guard stand out from the crowd.

96.8% Claim Ratio

Second highest claim settlement ratio among all private health insurers in India. Near-perfect claim reliability you can count on.

No Room Rent Limit

Choose any room category during hospitalisation. No proportional deductions on associated charges, unlike most competitors.

Cumulative Bonus up to 100%

Your sum insured doubles over claim-free years. Up to 100% NCB means a ₹10L policy effectively becomes ₹20L.

All Day-Care Procedures

Covers all listed day-care treatments that require less than 24-hour hospitalisation, including cataract, dialysis, and chemotherapy.

Domiciliary Hospitalisation

Covers treatment at home when hospital admission is advised but not possible due to medical condition or bed unavailability.

AYUSH Coverage

Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy treatments are covered at government-recognised AYUSH hospitals.

Convalescence Benefit

A lump sum payment if you are hospitalised for more than 10 consecutive days, covering incidental recovery expenses.

Critical Illness Rider

Optional add-on rider provides a lump sum payout on diagnosis of listed critical illnesses like cancer or heart attack.
