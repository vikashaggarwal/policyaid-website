Plan Highlights

What makes Star Comprehensive the go-to choice for complete family coverage

In-house Claim Settlement

No TPA involved. Claims are processed directly by Star Health, resulting in faster approvals and direct accountability.

Personal Accident Cover Included

Accidental death and permanent total disability cover up to the sum insured is bundled at no extra premium.

New-born Covered from Day 1

New-born babies are covered from the very first day of birth, including congenital conditions and neonatal care.

All Day-care Procedures

All IRDAI-listed day-care procedures covered without the need for 24-hour hospitalisation.

Domiciliary Hospitalisation

Treatment at home for conditions where hospitalisation is not feasible is covered, subject to doctor recommendation.

NRI Coverage

Non-resident Indians can purchase and maintain this policy, with coverage valid during visits to India.

No Room Rent Limit

Any room category can be chosen without proportionate deductions on the associated hospitalisation expenses.

Up to 100% No Claim Bonus

For every claim-free year, the sum insured increases. The NCB can accumulate up to 100% of the base sum insured.

Who Should Buy Star Comprehensive?

Profiles that are best matched to this plan

Families with New-borns or Expecting Parents

The Day 1 new-born cover is exceptional. Families who recently had or are expecting a baby avoid the waiting period issue that affects most plans.

Those Who Value Direct Claim Settlement

Policyholders who want to deal directly with the insurer rather than a TPA will appreciate Star's in-house processing model and faster resolutions.

Non-Resident Indians

NRIs who visit India regularly and want comprehensive health coverage during their stay can maintain this policy from abroad.

Risk-conscious Individuals Wanting Accident Cover

Getting personal accident cover bundled with health insurance at no extra cost represents strong value for working professionals and frequent travellers.

Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of Star Comprehensive

Advantages

  • In-house claim settlement - no TPA delays

  • Premium higher than Care Supreme for similar coverage
