Plan Highlights

The defining features that set HDFC Ergo Optima Restore apart

The Restore Benefit - India's Most Talked-about Feature

Unlike most plans that restore cover only for unrelated illnesses, Optima Restore restores 100% of your sum insured even if you are hospitalised for the same illness twice in a year. This makes it especially valuable for chronic condition sufferers or families with elderly members.

100% Restoration After First Claim

Once the sum insured is exhausted, it is restored fully - even for the same illness. No waiting for a second unrelated condition.

Multiplier Benefit

Your sum insured doubles after just 2 consecutive claim-free policy years at no extra premium, effectively giving you far more cover.

No Room Rent Sub-limits

Choose any hospital room category including a private suite without proportionate deductions on associated costs.

E-opinion for Critical Illness

Get a second medical opinion from leading specialists electronically for any critical illness diagnosis, at no extra cost.

No Sub-limits on Treatments

All modern treatments, surgical procedures, and ICU charges are covered without separate caps or sub-limits.

Home Health Care

Medically necessary treatment at home, recommended by a treating doctor, is covered under the plan.

96.1% Claim Settlement Ratio

HDFC Ergo settles 96.1% of all claims - one of the highest ratios in the Indian health insurance market.

Domiciliary Hospitalisation

Coverage for treatment at home for conditions where hospital admission is not feasible, per the doctor's recommendation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about HDFC Ergo Optima Restore

Once your base sum insured is exhausted due to a claim, Optima Restore automatically restores 100% of the original sum insured at no extra cost. The critical differentiator is that this restoration is available even for the same illness or injury - not just for unrelated conditions. So if you are hospitalised twice for the same heart condition in a year, both hospitalisations are covered.
The Multiplier Benefit increases your sum insured by 50% for each consecutive claim-free year, effectively doubling your cover after 2 years without any premium increase. For example, a ₹5 Lakh base cover grows to ₹7.5 Lakh after year 1 and ₹10 Lakh after year 2. The multiplier resets after a claim but begins accumulating again in subsequent claim-free years.
The maximum base sum insured under Optima Restore is ₹50 Lakh. While this is lower than competitors like Care Supreme (₹6 Crore), the Multiplier Benefit and Restore benefit together provide significantly higher effective coverage. A ₹50 Lakh plan with restoration effectively provides ₹1 Crore or more of coverage within a policy year.
HDFC Ergo Health Insurance has a claim settlement ratio of 96.1%, which is among the highest in the industry. This means that 96.1 out of every 100 valid claims submitted are settled successfully. This reflects the company's strong underwriting practices and commitment to honouring legitimate claims.
