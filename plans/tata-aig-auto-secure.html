Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of Tata AIG Auto Secure

Advantages

  • Widest selection of add-ons (20+) among major insurers
  • ₹15 Lakh owner-driver personal accident cover
  • EV-specific covers like Electrical Surge Cover
  • 7,500+ network garages backed by 650+ claim experts
  • 24x7 emergency assistance including hotel accommodation cover
  • Unique add-ons like Misfueling Cover and Glass/Fibre/Plastic Parts repair
  • Strong claim settlement ratio of approximately 95.4%

Disadvantages

  • Highest premium among comparable plans (~₹5,400/year) due to extensive add-on options
  • Many unique features require purchasing additional add-ons, increasing total cost
  • Claim ratio (~95.4%) is slightly behind HDFC ERGO and Digit
  • The sheer number of add-on choices can be overwhelming for first-time buyers
