Plan Highlights

What makes SBI General Private Car Package Policy stand out

India’s Largest Cashless Network

With 20,000+ network garages, SBI General offers the largest cashless repair network among all major motor insurers in India.

102.07% Incurred Claim Ratio

An incurred claim ratio above 100% means SBI General pays out more in claims than it collects in motor premiums - a strong sign for policyholders.

Backed by State Bank of India

As part of the SBI Group, SBI General brings the trust, scale and stability of India’s largest public sector bank to your motor insurance.

Strong Rural & Semi-Urban Reach

Leveraging SBI’s pan-India branch network, SBI General has exceptional reach in Tier 2, Tier 3 and rural areas where other insurers may have limited presence.

Zero Depreciation Add-on

Get full claim value on replaced parts without depreciation deductions, recommended for cars under 5 years old.

NCB Protect Cover

Retain your accumulated No Claim Bonus even after making one claim during the policy period.

Personal Accident Cover

Owner-driver personal accident cover included as part of the comprehensive policy, with options to enhance further.

24x7 Roadside Assistance

Round-the-clock support for breakdowns, towing, flat tyres and emergency fuel delivery anywhere in India.

Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of SBI General Private Car Package Policy

Advantages

  • India’s largest cashless garage network with 20,000+ garages
  • Incurred claim ratio of 102.07% - claims paid exceed premiums collected, favourable for policyholders
  • Backed by the trust and scale of State Bank of India
  • Exceptional reach in semi-urban and rural India
  • Comprehensive add-on suite including Zero Dep, Engine Protect, RTI
  • Standalone Own Damage and Third-Party variants also available
  • Personal Accident cover included as standard

Disadvantages

  • Premium (~₹5,200/year) is on the higher side compared to Bajaj Allianz or Royal Sundaram
  • Digital claims experience may not be as streamlined as Digit’s app-first approach
  • Add-ons like Zero Dep and RTI cost extra over the base premium
  • Customer service quality can vary across different SBI General branches
