Modular Plan Design

Add or remove coverage modules - OPD, global cover, mental health, wellness - based on your exact needs and budget. Pay only for what you use.

Unlimited Restoration

Sum insured restored an unlimited number of times per year once exhausted, ensuring continuous coverage without any cap on reinstatements.

Global Treatment Cover

Hospitalisation costs for treatment abroad covered, including major surgeries, transplants, and life-threatening conditions requiring overseas care.

OPD Cover Included

Doctor consultations, pharmacy bills, and diagnostic tests outside hospitalisation are covered without requiring a separate OPD rider purchase.

Mental Health Cover

In-patient psychiatric treatment and counselling sessions covered in compliance with IRDAI mental health parity regulations.

Wellness Rewards Program

Earn wellness points for healthy habits - gym visits, health check-ups, steps tracking - redeemable against premium discounts and health benefits.

No Room Rent Limit

Any room category at any network hospital is covered without proportionate deductions on doctor fees, surgery costs, or nursing charges.

No Co-payment

No co-payment clause applies. ICICI Lombard covers 100% of all eligible hospitalisation and treatment costs under the plan.
