Government-Backed Insurer

As a Government of India undertaking, New India Assurance offers the financial stability and backing that comes with public sector ownership.

Strong Tier 2/3 City Presence

Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of New India Assurance Private Car Insurance

Advantages

  • Over 100 years of operating history and institutional trust
  • Government of India backing provides strong financial stability
  • Competitive premium pricing (~₹4,700/year)
  • Strong presence in Tier 2, Tier 3 and rural areas
  • Standard add-ons available including Zero Dep, NCB Protect, RTI
  • Personal accident cover included with option to extend to co-passengers
  • Wide range of plan variants including Standalone OD and Third-Party only

Disadvantages

  • Smaller cashless garage network (3,000+) compared to SBI General or Reliance
  • Digital claims experience is less advanced than newer digital-first insurers
  • Claim settlement ratio (~96% estimate) is solid but not industry-leading
  • Branch-based service model may mean longer turnaround for some processes
