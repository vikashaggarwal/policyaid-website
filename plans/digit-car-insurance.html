Pros and Cons

An honest assessment of Go Digit Car Insurance

Advantages

  • Strong and improving claim settlement ratio of 97.2% (H1 FY26)
  • Zero-paperwork, fully digital claims process
  • Claims can be settled in as little as 7 minutes
  • ₹2,900+ crore in claims settled, demonstrating scale
  • Competitive premium - among the lower-cost comprehensive options
  • Comprehensive add-on suite including Zero Dep, Engine Protect, RTI
  • Easy policy management entirely through the Digit mobile app

Disadvantages

  • Smaller garage network (6,000+) compared to SBI General or Reliance
  • As a relatively newer insurer, fewer physical branch offices for in-person support
  • Self-inspection model may not suit customers who prefer in-person surveyor visits
  • Add-ons like Zero Dep and Engine Protect cost extra
