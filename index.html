Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Chat with us →

What is Policy Aid and how is it different from Policybazaar?
+
Policy Aid is a licensed IRDAI insurance agent focused on personalised service. Unlike aggregator websites, we assign you a dedicated advisor who helps you choose the right plan, assists during claims, and follows up until your claim is settled. We're not just a marketplace — we're your insurance partner.
Is it safe to buy insurance online through Policy Aid?
+
Absolutely. Policy Aid is an IRDAI-registered insurance agent. All payments are processed through encrypted, RBI-approved payment gateways. You receive your original policy document directly from the insurer via email — we never hold your policy documents.
How do I make a health insurance claim?
+
For cashless claims, simply go to a network hospital and show your e-insurance card. The hospital will coordinate directly with the insurer. For reimbursement claims, collect all bills, discharge summary and reports, then contact our claims team — we will guide you through every step and follow up with the insurer on your behalf.
Can I port my existing health insurance to a better plan?
+
Yes! Under IRDAI's portability guidelines, you can switch your health insurance to any insurer without losing your accumulated no-claim bonus or waiting period credits. Policy Aid's advisors specialise in porting — contact us at least 45 days before your renewal date.
What documents do I need to buy motor insurance online?
+
You need your vehicle's RC (Registration Certificate), your existing policy number (for renewal), and your driving licence. For new cars, the dealer typically provides the required documents. The entire process takes about 2 minutes on our platform.
Does travel insurance cover COVID-19 related cancellations?
+
Coverage varies by insurer and plan. Several of our partner insurers now offer plans that include medical treatment for COVID-19 abroad and trip cancellation due to a COVID-positive diagnosis. Our advisors can help you select a plan with the right coverage based on your destination.

Health Insurance Add-Ons

Upgrade Your Health Cover

These optional extras attach to your base health plan at a small additional premium. Pick only what your family actually needs — our advisors will guide you honestly.

Critical Illness Cover
Most Important

Diagnosed with cancer, heart attack, stroke or kidney failure? You receive a fixed lump sum — paid directly to you, no hospital bills needed. Use it for treatment, lost income, or recovery.

Best for: Anyone above 35 with a family history of serious illness
Maternity Cover

Covers delivery costs (normal & C-section), pre-natal care, and newborn treatment. Most plans have a 9-month to 2-year waiting period.

Add this early — before planning a pregnancy, not after
Room Rent Waiver

Many plans cap hospital rooms at ₹1,000–₹3,000/day. Choosing a better room reduces your entire claim — including doctor fees. This add-on removes that cap completely.

Without this, a room upgrade can cut your claim by 30–40%
OPD (Outpatient) Cover

Standard plans only cover hospital stays. OPD cover pays for doctor consultations, diagnostic tests, and medicines outside the hospital — expenses you have every month.

Ideal for families with young children or elderly parents
Hospital Cash Benefit

Get ₹500–₹5,000 cash per day while admitted. This is paid on top of your regular claim — use it for food, transport, or home help. Every day in hospital has hidden costs.

Especially useful for 3+ day hospitalisations
Global Treatment Cover

Extends your health cover for emergency hospitalisation anywhere in the world. If you travel frequently or want the option of treatment abroad, this is essential.

Best for frequent travellers and NRI families
Dental & Vision Cover

Covers dental treatment (fillings, extractions, root canals) and eye care (spectacles, lenses, eye surgery) — expenses that virtually all standard health plans exclude by default.

Starting from just ₹199/year — very affordable add-on
Ambulance Cover

Road and air ambulance costs covered in full. A road ambulance costs ₹2,000–₹5,000. An air ambulance can cost lakhs. Never avoid calling one because of the bill.

Critical in emergencies — don't let cost cause a delay
Consumables Cover

During any hospital stay, doctors use disposable items — syringes, gloves, PPE kits, bandages, and catheters. These can add ₹5,000–₹20,000 to your bill. Standard policies don't cover them. This add-on does.

Especially important for surgeries and ICU admissions
AYUSH Treatment Cover

Covers inpatient treatment under Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy. Many families prefer traditional medicine — this add-on ensures you're not out of pocket when you choose it.

Especially popular with senior citizens and families with chronic conditions
Personal Accident Rider

Accidental death or permanent disability pays a fixed lump sum directly to you or your family — separate from any hospital treatment claim. A financial safety net for life's worst moments.

Added to your health plan at a fraction of a standalone PA policy's cost

Motor Insurance Add-Ons

Upgrade Your Car Cover

A standard motor policy covers the basics. These add-ons fill the gaps that catch most car owners off guard when they finally make a claim.

Zero Depreciation
Must Have

When your car is repaired, insurers normally deduct 30–50% from your parts claim because parts "age." Zero-dep removes this deduction entirely — you get the full repair cost paid out.

Essential for cars under 5 years old. Saves ₹5,000–₹40,000 per claim
Return to Invoice (RTI)

If your car is stolen or totally destroyed, standard insurance pays today's market value — always lower than what you paid. RTI gives you the original invoice price instead.

Best for cars under 3 years old. Covers the full on-road price
Engine Protection Cover

Standard policies don't cover engine damage from waterlogging or oil leakage. Repairing or replacing an engine costs ₹50,000–₹3 lakhs — one of the most expensive car repairs.

Essential in Mumbai, Chennai & all flood-prone cities
NCB Protector

Made one claim? Normally your No-Claim Bonus resets to zero and your renewal premium jumps. NCB Protector lets you make one claim while keeping your accumulated discount intact.

Saves ₹3,000–₹8,000 on your next renewal premium
Consumables Cover

Every repair uses nuts, bolts, engine oil, coolant, and small parts. Standard policies don't pay for these — the garage bills you separately. This add-on covers them all.

Saves ₹1,000–₹5,000 on every repair visit
Key & Lock Replacement

Modern smart keys and key fobs cost ₹5,000–₹30,000 to replace and reprogram. If you lose your keys or they're stolen, this add-on covers the full replacement and lock-change cost.

A surprisingly expensive problem — covered for a small annual premium
Tyre Protection Cover

Standard policies don't cover tyre bursts, flat tyres, or sidewall damage unless caused directly by an accident. Tyre replacement on Indian roads is a common, costly surprise.

Particularly useful for SUVs and premium cars with expensive tyres
🧑‍⚕️
Personal Accident Cover
Law Required

If you're injured, permanently disabled, or killed in a car accident, a fixed compensation is paid — regardless of whose fault it was. IRDAI now mandates a minimum ₹15 lakh PA cover for every owner-driver.

Can be extended to cover passengers too — recommended for family cars
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Passenger Cover

The basic Personal Accident cover only protects the owner-driver. Passenger cover extends the same accident protection to all passengers in your car — compensation for injury, disability, or death.

Essential if you regularly carry family, colleagues, or hired drivers
Emergency Transport & Hotel

If your car breaks down far from home and repairs take over 24 hours, this add-on covers your hotel stay and travel back home. Particularly useful for highway and outstation travel.

Best for frequent outstation travellers and road trip enthusiasts
Daily Garage Cash

When your car is at a network garage for repairs after an accident, you get a fixed daily cash allowance — typically ₹500–₹1,500/day — to cover taxi or transport costs while you wait.

Covers up to 10–15 days depending on the plan chosen
